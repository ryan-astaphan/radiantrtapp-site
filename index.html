A calm companion for radiation therapy.

Log how you feel in under a minute. Learn what to expect each week. Unwind with guided audio tuned for RT.

What's inside

  • Daily check‑in — Five sliders. Energy, Skin, Pain, Mood, Sleep. Under a minute.
  • Week‑by‑week guides — Short, honest, plain language.
  • Guided audio — For procedural anxiety, fatigue, and sleep.
  • Your own trends — Simple graphs to share with your care team.

Free and premium

Radiant Essentials is free — it covers the symptom‑free early weeks.
Radiant Complete unlocks extended content and the full audio library when symptoms typically pick up around Week 3.

Monthly and annual options are shown inside the app. Managed entirely through your App Store / Google Play account.
