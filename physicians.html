For radiation oncology clinicians

A resource your patients can use between appointments.

Radiant RT Symptom Support is a patient-facing app built around the predictable arc of radiation therapy — with daily check-ins, week-by-week guidance, and RT-specific audio support. Created by a PA-C specializing in radiation oncology.

Built around the arc

Week-by-week guidance matched to where patients are in treatment — orienting them through the cumulative arc you already know well.

Evidence-informed

Educational content synthesized from a review of more than 90 clinical trials. Supporting research accepted for publication in Seminars in Radiation Oncology.

Visit-ready data

Patients can export PDF symptom-trend reports to bring to appointments — giving care teams a clearer picture of what's been happening between visits.
